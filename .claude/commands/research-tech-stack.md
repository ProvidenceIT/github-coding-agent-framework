# Technology Stack Research

Research optimal technology choices for your project based on requirements, scale, and constraints.

## What I'll Research

### Frontend Technologies
- **Frameworks**: React, Vue, Svelte, Angular
- **Styling**: Tailwind, CSS-in-JS, SASS
- **State Management**: Redux, Zustand, Recoil
- **Build Tools**: Vite, Webpack, esbuild

### Backend Technologies
- **Frameworks**: Express, Fastify, NestJS, Flask, FastAPI
- **Runtime**: Node.js, Python, Go, Rust
- **APIs**: REST, GraphQL, tRPC

### Database Options
- **SQL**: PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite
- **NoSQL**: MongoDB, Redis, DynamoDB
- **ORMs**: Prisma, TypeORM, SQLAlchemy

### Infrastructure
- **Hosting**: Vercel, AWS, DigitalOcean, Railway
- **Authentication**: Auth0, Clerk, Supabase Auth
- **Storage**: S3, Cloudinary, UploadThing

## Research Criteria

I'll evaluate based on:
- ✅ Project requirements and scale
- ✅ Team expertise and learning curve
- ✅ Performance requirements
- ✅ Budget constraints
- ✅ Deployment complexity
- ✅ Community support and ecosystem
- ✅ Long-term maintainability

## Output

You'll receive:
- **Recommended stack** with rationale
- **Alternative options** with tradeoffs
- **Learning resources** for chosen technologies
- **Integration examples** and best practices
- **Estimated complexity** and timeline

Provide your project details:

I'll research and recommend the optimal stack for your needs.
